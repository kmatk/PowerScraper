On the occasions where a kingdom is in war/fight, high powered active governors may be included in a day's data before the war/fight, but fallen out of top 300 power after the war/fight day.
Hence this is NOT suitable for measure KvK kingdom participation and performance! Please visit our FAQ page for detailed explanation.
